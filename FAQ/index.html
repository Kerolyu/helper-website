Frequently asked questions

Can I schedule monthly cleaning?

Yes, you can set up a schedule for weekly,bi-weekly or monthly cleaning.

How Far in Advance Can I Book My Task?

Yes, you can set up a schedule for weekly,bi-weekly or monthly cleaning.

What Should I Do if I forgot to update the address?

If an incorrect address was entered when booking and the correct task address is close by, we suggest keeping the task active and sending a message to your Tasker using that task's chat thread to inform them of the correct address.

How can I be sure about the cleaner's credibility?

All our cleaners go through verification check and we as a company take some of the responsibility as well.

Can I book a Same Day Task?

You can book same day tasks by changing the date to "Today" while viewing potential Taskers.
